Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

TL;DR: TokenFlow is designed with privacy in mind. We process data locally in your browser, don't collect personal information, and don't transmit your conversations to external servers.

Information We Collect

TokenFlow operates entirely within your browser and processes information locally. Here's what happens:

  • Local Processing: We analyze text content on AI chat pages (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok) to count tokens
  • Settings Data: Your preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or conversation content
  • No Account Required: The extension works without any registration or login

How We Use Information

All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Token counting and context window analysis
  • Displaying real-time usage statistics
  • Remembering your theme preferences and settings
  • Providing platform-specific optimizations

Data Storage

TokenFlow stores minimal data locally in your browser:

  • Extension Settings: Theme preferences, display options, and configuration stored in Chrome's local storage
  • No Cloud Storage: No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • No Conversation History: We don't store or remember your AI conversations

Third-Party Services

TokenFlow interacts with AI platforms in a read-only manner:

  • Content Analysis: We read page content to identify conversation text and count tokens
  • No Data Transmission: We don't send any data to these platforms or any external services
  • Platform Compatibility: We adapt our interface to work with different AI platforms' themes and layouts

Your Privacy Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control:

  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • Settings Reset: You can reset all preferences through the extension options
  • No Tracking: We don't use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies

Security

We implement security best practices:

  • All processing happens locally in your browser's secure environment
  • No network connections for data transmission
  • Minimal permissions requested (only active tab access for supported AI platforms)
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Open Source

TokenFlow is open source, which means:

  • You can review our code to verify our privacy claims
  • The community can audit our security practices
  • Transparency in how we handle data and implement features

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users through the extension if changes are significant
  • Maintain previous versions for reference
